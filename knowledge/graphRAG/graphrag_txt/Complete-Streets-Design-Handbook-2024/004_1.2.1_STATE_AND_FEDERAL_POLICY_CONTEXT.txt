1.2.1	STATE AND FEDERAL POLICY CONTEXT
The Philadelphia Complete Streets Design Handbook is intended to supplement 
rather than replace existing engineering and design standards, including but not 
limited to the Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices (MUTCD). Some of the 
design treatments described in this Handbook are not directly referenced in the 
current editions of these documents, but best practices are provided based on
