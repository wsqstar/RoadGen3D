Intersections should be designed to provide a safe and comfortable path for 
people biking through the intersection. At intersections or driveways, vehicles and 
bikes may cross paths. Bicycle conflict zone markings alert bicycles and drivers of 
potential conflict and help delineate the appropriate right-of-way.
